About

Seth Holladay is a software engineer, music producer, and entrepreneur. As an open source enthusiast, most of his work is available freely to the public.

For more than 10 years, Seth has been working to simplify the world through intuitive apps built on reusable libraries and tools. Seth has founded companies in the music and technology fields, including Chords and Replay Avenue. He also helped to create Sitecues, assistive technology for people with disabilities.

Some of Seth's hobbies include photography and old video games.
